Southern style sweet potato casserole

Sue

Ingredients

Potato Mash:

  • 4 C. mashed sweet potatoes
  • ½ C. white sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • ½ t. salt
  • 4 T. softened butter
  • ½ C. milk
  • ½ t. vanilla extract

Topping:

  • ½ C. brown sugar, packed
  • ⅓ C. all-purpose flour
  • 3 T. softened butter
  • ½ C. chopped pecans

Directions

Potato Mash:

  1. Cook sweet potatoes at 425° F for 90 minutes.
  2. Peel and mash potatoes.
  3. Mix potatoes, sugar, eggs, salt, butter, milk, and vanilla.
  4. Place into 9x13 baking dish.

Topping:

  1. Mix sugar and flour. Cut in butter until mixture is coarse.
  2. Stir in pecans. Sprinkle over Potato Mash.
