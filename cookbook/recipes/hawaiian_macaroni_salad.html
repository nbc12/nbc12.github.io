Hawaiian Macaroni Salad

Cook time 8-10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ C. milk
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • ½ C. mayo
  • 2 C. elbow macaroni
  • ¼ C. grated onion
  • 1 C. shredded carrot
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Cook elbow macaroni for 8-10 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, combine apple cider vinegar, salt, milk, sugar, and mayo in a mixing bowl.
  3. Drain and rinse macaroni with cold water, then add to the mixing bowl containing dressing.
  4. Add grated onion, shredded carrot, and salt and pepper to taste. Mix and serve.
