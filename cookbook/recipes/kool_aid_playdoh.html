Kool-Aid Playdoh

Erin Christensen

Ingredients

  • 2 c. water
  • 3 T. oil
  • 2 pkg Kool-Aid
  • 2 ½ c. flour stir in after removing from heat
  • ½ c. salt stir in after removing from heat

Directions

  1. In a large sauce pot, bring to a boil: water, oil, and Kool-Aid.
  2. Remove from heat.
  3. With a wooden spoon, stir in flour and salt.
