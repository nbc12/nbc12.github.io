Crisco Chocolate Cake

Christensen Cookbook

Yield Makes 2 9” layers or 1 9”x13” pan.

Ingredients

  • 2 C. + 2 T. flour
  • ½ t. baking powder
  • 1 ⅔ C. sugar
  • ½ C. shortening (Crisco)
  • 1 ½ t. baking soda
  • 1 t. salt
  • 1 C. milk
  • 3 square unsweetened chocolate (9 T. cocoa)
  • 3 eggs, unbeaten
  • 1 ½ t. vanilla
  • ⅔ C. milk
  • ½ t. red food color

Directions

  1. Mix thoroughly 2 minutes at medium speed on mixer.
  2. Add eggs, vanilla, ⅔ cup milk, and red food color. Beat 2 minutes more.
  3. Bake at 350° F for 40 minutes.
