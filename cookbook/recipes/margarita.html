Margarita

Blendtec lifestyles recipe book

Ingredients

  • 3 T. Margarita mix
  • 1 T. Triple Sec
  • 1 T. sweetened lime juice
  • ¼ C. lemonade
  • 1 ½ C. ice cubes

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a shaker or large jar with ice. Shake or stir vigorously until well chilled. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
