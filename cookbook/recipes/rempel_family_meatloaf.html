Rempel Family Meatloaf

https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/20675/rempel-family-meatloaf/

Yield 6 servings Prep time 10 minutes Cook time 45 minutes Total time 55 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ lb. lean ground beef
  • ½ C. crushed buttery round crackers
  • ¾ C. shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 1 oz. package dry onion soup mix
  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ C. ketchup
  • 2 T. steak sauce

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350° F (175° C).
  2. Stir the ground beef, crushed crackers, Cheddar cheese, and onion soup mix in a large bowl until well combined. Whisk the eggs, ketchup, and steak sauce in a separate bowl until smooth. Mix the eggs into the meat until evenly combined, if the mixture seems too dry, add a little water. Press into a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  3. Bake in preheated oven until the meatloaf reaches 160° F (71° C) and is no longer pink in the center, 45 to 60 minutes.
