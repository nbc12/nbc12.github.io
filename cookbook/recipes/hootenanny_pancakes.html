Hootenanny Pancakes

Christensen Cookbook

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 1 ½ t. salt
  • 1 cube margarine
  • 1 C. flour
  • 1 C. milk

Directions

  1. Combine eggs, milk and salt in blender container, beat until fluffy.
  2. Tap in flour a little at a time, beating until well blended.
  3. Melt margarine in 9x13 baking dish in oven.
  4. When butter is melted, pour in batter.
  5. Bake 425° F for 20-25 min.
