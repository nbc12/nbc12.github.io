Russian Punch

Christensen Cookbook

Yield 1 Gallon

Ingredients

  • 2 ⅔ C. Sugar
  • 2 ⅔ C. Orange Juice
  • 2 qt. Water
  • 1 ⅓ t. Almond Extract
  • 2 ⅔ C. Water
  • 1 C. Lemon Juice
  • 2 ⅔ t. Vanilla

Directions

  1. Simmer together sugar and 2 c. Water of the 2 ⅔ c. for 5 minutes.
  2. Combine with remaining ingredients and either chill for a cold punch or heat to boiling and serve warm.
