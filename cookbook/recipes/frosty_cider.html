Frosty Cider

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ C. apple cider
  • ¼ C. apple Schnapps
  • ½ large apple
  • ½ large orange
  • 1 ¼ C. vanilla ice cream
  • ½ t. cinnamon
  • ½ t. nutmeg

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender or mixing bowl and blend or whisk until smooth. Serve immediately.
