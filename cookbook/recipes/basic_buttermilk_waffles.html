Basic Buttermilk Waffles

Christensen Cookbook

Yield 5 generous servings

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs, separated
  • 3 C. buttermilk
  • 1 ½ t. baking soda
  • 3 C. flour
  • 3 t. baking powder
  • ¾ t. salt
  • ½ C. + 1 T. oil
  • 3 T. sugar

Directions

  1. Beat egg whites until stiff. Set aside.
  2. Beat egg yolks, then beat in remaining ingredients.
  3. Fold in egg whites.
  4. Bake in waffle iron.
