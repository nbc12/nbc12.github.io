Creamed Eggs

Christensen Cookbook

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • 6 eggs
  • 2 C. white sauce
  • Paprika

Directions

  1. Hard boil the eggs. When cooked, place in pan of cold water.
  2. Meanwhile, make approx. 2 c. white sauce.
  3. Peel and chop eggs and add to white sauce.
  4. Serve over toast and garnish with paprika.
